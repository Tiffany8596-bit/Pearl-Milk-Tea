Bubble Tea Started Here in Taiwan

Back in the late 1980s, a little tea shop in Taichung, Taiwan, called Chun Shui Tang, tried something new. They decided to mix their creamy milk tea with chewy little tapioca pearls, just to see what would happen. People loved it! The drink was fun to sip, sweet and creamy, with the pearls giving it a playful twist. And just like that, bubble tea was born.

Over time, this fun little invention started to spread. People told their friends about it, other tea shops tried making it too, and before long, bubble tea became something everyone wanted to try. More and more brands popped up, and eventually, it grew beyond Taiwan, reaching tea lovers all around the world.
